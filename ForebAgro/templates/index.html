Welcome to Foreb Agro!

Foreb Agro Products is a home-grown company dedicated primarily to the processing of oil palm kernel into Palm Kernel Oil (PKO) and Palm Kernel Cake (PKC) for use as intermediates in further production processes. Our mission is to ensure that local demand for food products are satisfied through locally sourced inputs and resources rather than imports. To this end, we have developed a clear, consistent framework to deliver quality products by empowering and building relationships with local farmers and other members of our value chain.

OUR SUPPLY CHAIN

We supply our products to customers both within and outside the shores of Nigeria. Our fleet of delivery trucks allows us to deliver to customers within Nigeria, and our export expertise, which has developed immensely over the years, enables us provide our products to customers abroad...

OUR PRODUCTS

We are dedicated solely to processing Oil Palm kernel into Palm Kernel Oil (PKO) and Palm Kernel Cake (PKC) for use as intermediates in further production processes. At Foreb Agro, we have a clear, consistent framework to deliver quality products and services...

OUR PROCESS

At Foreb Agro, our major raw material (Oil Palm kernel) is sourced locally, primarily from Western and Central Nigeria. On arrival, we test these materials for quality, oil content and nutritional value at our laboratory...


We provide a wide range of  palm-kernel produce to markets locally and internationally. Our networks and services enable us to maintain a strong position in the palm kernel trading market and provide the best prices and services to our customers.
